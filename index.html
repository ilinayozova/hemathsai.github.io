HE Maths AI is a workshop part of the IMA’s Higher Education Teaching and Learning Series for 2025/26 to be held at University College London (UCL) on Monday, 1 June 2026.

The workshop seeks to catalyse research at the interface of Bayesian statistics and artificial intelligence. Themes include:
  • Generative modeling.
  • Physics-informed machine learning models.
  • Monte Carlo methods in machine learning.
  • Applications of the above.
Call for submissions: We invite participants to submit posters for the evening session on Monday, 23 September. See the registration form for more details.

The registration deadline is 7th September. The registration is closed.

Code of Conduct: The Prob_AI Hub has a commitment to the promotion of a supportive and inclusive culture and the development of a diverse community in probabilistic AI. See the Code of Conduct for the Hub’s expectations of acceptable behaviour in its activities.
